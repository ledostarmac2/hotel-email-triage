## 9. Operational SOPs

### PG (Pre-Guarantee) / No-Show Process
- Reservations without a valid credit card or deposit are flagged as "PG"
- No-show policy: 1 night room + tax charged if guest fails to arrive without cancelling within policy window
- Front Office (Marina/Renee/Noah) handles no-show billing disputes
- Finance (Areum Jo) handles folio reconciliation for disputed charges

### OOO (Out-Of-Order) Room Policy
- Rooms taken OOO cannot be sold; Pre-Arrival (Catherine Esposo) manages OOO communications
- Guests booked in OOO rooms must be proactively contacted and moved with compensation
- Standard comp: room upgrade + welcome amenity + apology letter signed by FOM

### IPO vs. Master Bill
- **IPO** = Individual Pay Own — each room charged individually to the guest's card
- **Master Bill** = Group master account; group coordinator pays centrally via Sertifi or wire transfer
- Finance (Areum Jo) manages master bill setup, wire confirmations, and folio reconciliation

### Group Room Blocks / Attrition
- Group contracts include attrition clauses (typically 80–90% pickup required before cutoff date)
- Adrien Aloi Haley manages BEOs (Banquet Event Orders) and group billing
- Jenna Fisco manages rooming lists and room block pickup reports
- Passkey portal allows group guests to self-book within the block

### Upgrade / Upsell SOP
- Upgrades processed by Reservations team (Brian/Chris/Dakota)
- Complimentary upgrades for Hilton Diamond, Centurion, and FHR bookings
- Premium suite upsells routed to David Martins for approval
- VIP upgrades coordinated between Sales and Pre-Arrival

### VIP Arrival Process
- Pre-Arrival Manager (Catherine Esposo) coordinates all VIP arrivals
- VIP amenity orders placed 24–48 hrs before arrival
- Welcome letters drafted by Pre-Arrival; approved by Front Office Manager on duty
- High-profile VIPs (celebrities, executives) escalated to David Martins or Luigi Romaniello
